The Plot

Set in 1919, Birmingham, England, right after the First World War, the ambitious razor gang leader of the Peaky Blinders, Thomas Shelby finds himself in a situation, where he has to hide a big supply of stolen guns, from Inspector Campbell.

The Inspector was sent by Winston Churchill, this helps to make the show feel more realistic.

Will Thomas and his mob succeed in hiding the guns from the Inspector or will they fail?

Main Characters

Thomas Shelby

Thomas 'Tommy' Shelby, comes from a gypsie family. where his father is Arthur Shelby. He's also the brother of Arthur, John, Ada and Finn Shelby, and he is the leader of the Peaky Blinders. Traumatized by The Great War, where he was fighting in the trenches along his brothers, he came back as fully grown man who barely smiles anymore.

Inspector Campbell

The Cheif Inspector, is a trusted man of the British Government. He built his reputation in Belfast, Ireland, where he was tasked with ridding the city of crime and corruption. He did not fight in The First World War, to which Thomas Shelby will refer multiple times in the series.

Polly Gray

Elizabeth Gray, also known as Polly, is the aunt of the Shelby brothers, she helps guiding the family in harder times by being the wisest amongst them.

Arthur and John Shelby

Arthur being the oldest, and John being the youngest of the three, they are not as smart as the middle brother Tommy, but they are strong. Their strenght helps the criminal mastermind carry out the missions and objectives in order to succeed.

Grace Helen

Grace was an Irish barmaid who took up employment at The Garrison Pub, and secretly operated as an undercover agent for the Birmingham Police. Her mission involved getting close to Thomas Shelby whom she fell in love with. He too returned the sentiment but was heartbroken when he discovered her treachery.

Spoilers for Seasons 1 - 5.

Season 1

After Tommy Shelby decides to keep the guns for leverage, he later strikes up a deal with Campbell. Later on, Grace goes to Campbell and replaces the information about guns in order to ensure Tommy's safety. She then gets a marriage proposal from Campbell but it is later revealed that she is in love with Tommy.

Season 2

Season 2 begins with Freddie's funeral, who was Ada's boyfriend (later husband) after he passes away due to Spanish flu. On the other hand, Grace attempts to shoot Campbell but he escapes death and returns to Birmingham. Later on, Alfie and Sabini (gang leaders in London) collaborate to murder Arthur's men while Grace returns to Tommy after she gets married and reveals that she is pregnant.

Season 3

It begins with a backdrop of 1924 when Tommy and Grace get married while Mr. Romanov (russian fugitive) pays gratitude to Tommy for killing the impostor. Furthermore, Grace gets shot and killed at the Shelby Charity Foundation dinner while the Grand Duchess Tatiana distributes it among the Shelby family. However, the police later arrive and arrest them all.

Season 4

The fourth season of the show further follows the story of the Shelby family who is released from prison. Each of the members then receives a letter from the Sicilian mafia revealing that they'd been marked for death for murdering Angel and Vincente. On the other hand, Tommy betrays Jessie Eden (communist party leader) and becomes a Labour MP in Birmingham South.

Season 5

Season 5 begins in 1929 followed by the events of the financial crisis, where they lose a very big amount of money, because Michael failed to do what Tommy asked of him. The story then revolves around Tommy Shelby's encounter with a politician and how his response will affect the future of the entire nation. Also Thomas believes that somebody wants his throne, somebody is going to betray him.

What will happen in the 6th and final season?

After Tommy fails to stop fascist party leader Oswald Mosley, and also losing important members of the family, goes crazy and tries to kill himself, but will he pull the trigger and leave his family behind? Well, we will have to wait for season 6 to be released to know.
